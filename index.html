THE WORLD CULTURE FESTIVAL 2022- EGYPT





The Arab Republic of Egypt covers an area of 1 million km², which is about twice the size of France or more than twice the size of the US State of California. With 102.2 million inhabitants (2021), Egypt is the most populous country in the Arab world. The capital and largest city is Cairo. Spoken language is Modern Standard Arabic. Is most famous for the Nile River, which is considered the longest river in the world and the life source of the country. Also, for the Great Pyramids of Giza, the greatest royal tombs ever built, and the Great Sphinx, the enormous ancient Egyptian stone monument on the Giza Plateau with a lion's body and a human head.


Some interesting facts about the country:

  • It is a transcontinental country, which means that it is ubicated in both Africa (northeast) and Asia (southwest).
  • Great care was taken in the mummification process because the ancient Egyptians believed that the body needed to be preserved to be reborn and be ferried to the afterlife. They even mummified animals./li>
  • Cleopatra, one of the most famous figures from ancient Egypt, is actually Greek, not Egyptian.
